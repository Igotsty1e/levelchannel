# AI Workflow

## Product usage

The core runtime is not AI-driven. Checkout, payment handling, consent capture, and account flows are deterministic server-side logic.

## Development usage

AI is used during development for:

- implementation support
- documentation drafting
- research and review assistance

## Where AI is not trusted

- payment decisions
- webhook verification
- legal consent capture
- security-critical request validation

## Human role

Humans retain responsibility for architecture, compliance, operational procedures, and any shipped behavior in the payment and auth surfaces.

## Guiding rule

AI may assist the team, but it does not own the trust boundary.
